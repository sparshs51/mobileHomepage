Prepp-IAS GS Pre cum Main Foundation course is a uniquely designed course for comprehensive coverage of every aspect of both the Preliminary and Mains stage of UPSC Civil Services Examination. The course is conducted by young and dynamic educators who are well versed with the emerging demand of USPC CSE and understand the nerve of the exam. This course is spread over approximately 18 months and 1000+ hours of live classes. Along with regular classes, it also includes strategy sessions, doubt clearing sessions, test series (both mains and prelims), and mentorship sessions to ensure holistic preparation for UPSC CSE. The course also covers current affairs on a weekly, fortnightly, and monthly basis. In addition, intensive sessions on improving answer writing and essay writing skills are conducted to develop and inculcate better writing skills.

The objective of this course is to be a one-stop destination for your needs regarding the UPSC CSE 2023 examination. The course aims to provide genuine guidance, right approach, and relevant content to maximize your chances of clearing the UPSC CSE examination.
